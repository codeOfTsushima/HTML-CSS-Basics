How to Make Classic Pancakes

  1. Mix flour,sugar,baking powder, and salt in bowl.
  2. In another bowl,whisl milk,eggs, and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients.
  4. Pour batter onto a hot griddle.
  5. Flip when bubbles form and cook until golden.

Ingredients for Panckakes

Ingredient Quantity
flour 1 cup
Milk 3/4 cup
Eggs 2
Sugar 2 tbsp
